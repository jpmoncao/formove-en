We, developers of the ForMove application, are a group of students from Escola Técnica Professor Armando José Farinazzo from Fernandopolis, São Paulo.

The application was idealized, due to the need to create a scientific initiation project that address the issue of health.

So we put this old idea, designed in late 2021, into execution.

We would like to thank the support of the guiding professors: Joel Baptista, Flavia Cotrim and Geici Mara.

The students involved in this project, both in terms of research and development, are the result of a teamwork, a large team of ten members, they are: João Pedro Monção, Ariel Aio, Davi Ribeiro, Arthur Rodovalho, Mariana Molina, Amanda Pupim, Jhenifer Laila, Cinthia Zague, Gabriel Aguera and Higor Donda.
