Physical Activity

Writer by Gabriel Aguera

2022/11/09

1. Need to practice activities

The practice of physical activity is undoubtedly essential to increase our quality of life. Countless studies demonstrate how the frequent practice of physical activity prevents diseases and even improves our disposition to carry out our daily activities

Physical activities generate a great amount of benefits for our body and also for our mind. Some benefits of practicing physical activities are:

  • Physical activity prevents the development of chronic diseases such as hypertension and diabetes;
  • Physical activities control cholesterol levels;
  • Physical activity can be an important ally in the treatment of depression and anxiety;
  • Physical activity improves muscular and also cardiorespiratory conditioning;
  • Physical activity causes a series of changes in the body, such as muscle mass gain, which is directly related to the improvement of self-esteem;
  • Physical activity is important for weight control;
  • Physical activity improves pain and decreases functional disability;
  • The practice of physical activities improves the quality of sleep;
  • As atividades físicas melhoram o desempenho cognitivo;
  • The practice of physical activities reduces stress and increases the feeling of well-being.

2. Recommendations and tips

According to the World Health Organization, the recommendation for practice is 150 minutes of weekly light or moderate physical activity, which corresponds to a little more than 20 daily minutes of activities. About 75 minutes per week of higher intensity physical activities may correspond to just over 10 minutes a day

Including physical exercises in a routine is difficult, but it is extremely important for health. Faced with this need, the Ministry of Health gives four important tips to start the practice of physical activities:

  • Find a suitable place to practice physical activities, such as parks, squares and the like;
  • Start with an activity that does not require high physical fitness;
  • Practicing physical activity close to home, not requiring large displacements, which helps in maintaining this habit;
  • Look for activities performed by several people, including your circle of friends, which could be an extra stimulus.
